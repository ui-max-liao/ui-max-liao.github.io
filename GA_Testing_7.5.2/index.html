P0 Bug: PoE Total Power Limit Negative Value (7.5.2)

▼

Signed/unsigned 32-bit integer overflow in PoE driver. Affects BCM5616x and BCM5334x platforms. RTL93xx, RTL838x, and MT7621 are unaffected. Bug persists from 7.5.0.

Impact: All BCM-based PoE switches (BCM5616x, BCM5334x) report negative Total Power Limit and per-port PwrLimit of -1 via swctrl poe show. This is a 32-bit signed integer overflow (~0xBE...). Actual PoE delivery may still function but budget enforcement and reporting are broken.

Finding: swctrl stp Subcommand Removed in 7.5.2

▼

The swctrl stp show command is no longer available on 8 of 9 platforms in 7.5.2. Only MT7621 (USW Flex) retains it.
